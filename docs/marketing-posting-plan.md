Phase 1: Launch week (post every day)
-------------------------------------

For the next 7–10 days while you push the LinkedIn SSI Booster:

* Post **1 Short every day**.

* Use a **hybrid timing strategy** so you learn fast:
  
  * 3–4 posts in your **old “morning” windows** (9Tue/9Thu/11Sat mornings).
  
  * 3–4 posts in **Buffer’s suggested evening windows** (Thu 7–8 p.m., Fri 6–7 p.m., Sat 7–8 p.m.).

* Keep topics tightly tied to:
  
  * SSI Booster, social selling, AI‑assisted content, Java/AI engineer angle.

Track per Short (first 24h): views, watch time, likes, subs gained. You only need a simple sheet.
Phase 2: After launch (lock in a rhythm)
----------------------------------------

After 1–2 weeks of daily posts:

* Look at which **day+time combos** gave you the best 24h performance.

* Move to a sustainable **3–4 Shorts/week** cadence:
  
  * Keep **1–2 morning slots** (when you naturally batch/record).
  
  * Keep **2 evening slots** that clearly performed best from the test.[](https://stockimg.ai/blog/best-time-to-post-on-youtube/best-time-to-post-videos-and-shorts-on-youtube)

That gives you:

* Enough volume for the product launch.

* Data to justify changes instead of blindly trusting Buffer.

* A long‑term schedule that mixes “builder mornings” with “scrolling evenings.”
